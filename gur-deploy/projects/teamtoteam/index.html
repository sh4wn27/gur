Overview

The 1-on-1 Robotics Mentorship program connects younger students with experienced FTC and FRC team members for personalized guidance in robotics, engineering fundamentals, and problem-solving. The goal is to support students not just technically, but also academically and personally as they navigate STEM for the first time.

Problem —

Problem —

Problem —

Many students interested in robotics lack access to mentors who can provide consistent, individualized support. Large classrooms, limited school resources, and competition-focused environments often leave beginners feeling overwhelmed or discouraged.

Result —

Result —

Result —

Through direct mentorship, students gain confidence, foundational technical skills, and a clearer understanding of engineering pathways. Mentors also develop leadership and teaching experience, creating a sustainable cycle of learning within the robotics community.
