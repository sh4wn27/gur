How we work

Our approach is built around mentorship, accessibility, and hands-on learning—meeting students where they are and helping them grow through real robotics experiences.

1. Understand

We start by understanding each student or team’s background, experience level, and goals—whether they are brand new to robotics or preparing for competitive seasons.
